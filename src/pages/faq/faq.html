FAQ Q: Where does this data come from?
A: All data is available through the Spotify Web API. uTrack doesn't do any independent data collecting of its own.
Q: Can you show more date options?
A: Unfortunately not. Spotify only provides data for the 3 current date ranges.
Q: Can you show a play count for my top tracks?
A: Play count info isn't available, I'd love to include it if I could!
